About Novello Stone

Built on the tools, not just the spreadsheet.

Novello Stone is an owner-operated stone and surface solutions business based in Mossel Bay, serving the Garden Route corridor from Hartenbos to George.

The business is run on direct hands-on experience in fabrication, tiling, and restoration — not managed at arm's length. Between us, the team carries over 15 years of combined, tool-in-hand experience in stone. Every quote is priced against real operating rates, and every job runs to the same standard: measure properly, quote straight, and finish what's started.

We run this like a family business, because that's what it is — no call centres, no account managers, just the people doing the work. Not sure whether something's a fabrication job, a tiling job, or a repair? Ask us anyway. We'd rather help you find the right fit than watch you guess.
