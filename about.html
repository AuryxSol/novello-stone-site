About Novello Stone

Built on the tools, not just the spreadsheet.

Novello Stone is a wholly-owned, independently registered stone and surface solutions business based in Mossel Bay, serving the Garden Route corridor from Hartenbos to George.

The business is owner-operated, run on direct hands-on experience in fabrication, tiling, and restoration — not managed at arm's length. Every quote is priced against real operating rates, and every job is run with the same standard: measure properly, quote straight, and finish what's started.
