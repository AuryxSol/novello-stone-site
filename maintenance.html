Maintenance Packages

Every property wears stone differently.

A poolside lappa and a hotel lobby don't need the same care plan. Tell us what you've got, and we'll build a maintenance schedule around it — not the other way around.

Maintenance is different from restoration. Restoration is a one-off fix for stone that's already worn, etched, or damaged. Maintenance is the ongoing plan that keeps it from getting there again — scheduled cleaning, sealing, and inspection, tailored to how the space is actually used.
