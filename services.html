Restoration

Grind-and-repolish, sealing, repairs, and ongoing maintenance contracts.

Natural stone loses its finish over time — traffic wear, etch marks, dulling, and chips. Restoration brings it back without replacing the surface. We also run scheduled maintenance contracts for estates, hospitality venues, and commercial premises that need stone to keep performing year-round.
