Services

One business, three trades, no gaps between them.

Fabrication, tiling, and restoration are usually three separate quotes from three separate contractors. We run all three under one standard of measurement, costing, and finish.

Marble Granite Engineered Stone Quartzite Porcelain Slab

Fabrication

Bridge saw operations and slab work for countertops, vanities, and custom stone surfaces.

Every fabrication job starts with an accurate template and ends with a fitted, finished surface. We work in marble, engineered stone (Caesarstone-type material), and quartzite, with slab material costed and billed separately from labour so the client always sees exactly what they're paying for.

Why the site visit matters: Stone fabrication pricing depends on slab availability, seam placement, edge profile, and existing cabinetry or substrate condition — none of which can be priced accurately from a phone call. A short site visit gets you an exact, itemised quote instead of a wide guess.
  • On-site templating and measurement
  • Edge profiling and custom cut-outs
  • Coordination of slab sourcing
  • Professional installation and final fit
Pricing: Quoted after a site visit. Slab material is billed separately from labour at confirmed supplier cost, shown as its own line item.
Porcelain Ceramic Mosaic Large-Format Natural Stone Tile

Tiling

Residential and commercial tiling across all formats, laid to fall and finished to last.

From bathroom retiles to large-format commercial floors, the work is measured and set out properly before a single tile goes down. We also take on contract tiling work through builders and retail partners across the corridor.

Why the site visit matters: Tiling cost depends on substrate condition, falls and drainage in wet areas, tile format and pattern, and site access — all things that change the labour required. A site visit lets us quote the job in front of us, not an average job.
  • All tile formats, residential and commercial
  • Proper substrate prep and waterproofing where required
  • Accurate falls for wet areas
  • Grouting, sealing, and snag-free finish
Pricing: Quoted after a site visit. Material is billed separately from labour, shown as its own line item.
Marble Granite Terrazzo Natural Stone

Restoration

Grind-and-repolish, sealing, repairs, and ongoing maintenance contracts.

Natural stone loses its finish over time — traffic wear, etch marks, dulling, and chips. Restoration brings it back without replacing the surface. We also run scheduled maintenance contracts for estates, hospitality venues, and commercial premises that need stone to keep performing year-round.

Why the site visit matters: The right restoration process depends on stone type, how worn or etched the surface is, and whether previous coatings or sealers are present. Assessing this in person avoids over-quoting (or under-quoting) the grinding stages needed.
  • Diamond grinding and multi-stage polishing
  • Chip and crack repair
  • Professional-grade sealing
  • Scheduled maintenance for estates and commercial sites
Pricing: Quoted after a site visit. Sealer is always billed as a separate line item from labour, regardless of project size.
